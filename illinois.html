Project Onward

Paintings

Project Onward is a non-profit organization that launched in 2004 located in Chicago, IL. It is dedicated to allowing disabled artists the opportunity to tell their stories. They have created an environment that is easy to access, supportive, safe, and free of charge to join. Project Onward connects these artists with the general public to reduce the stigma surrounding mental illness and disabilities. They hold professional exhibitions and sell their work to emphasize the economic and social value, there are currently more than 60 artists being supported by the organization. Project Onward will continue to support its artists through financial contributions and volunteerism.

Hyde Park School of Dance

Dance

Hyde Park School of Dance is a non-profit organization located in Chicago that is committed to give back to their neighborhood and community structured dance opportunities. They offer lecture demonstrations, in-school dance supplements, after-school programming, and neighborhood performances. Hyde Park School of Dance offers a variety of different styles of dance including pre ballet, ballet, modern, jazz, and hip-hop with programs for different age ranges.

Music Institute of Chicago

Music

Music institute of Chicago is a non profit organization founded in 1931, it is dedicated to providing music lessons and classes. Their main goal is to provide a lifelong engagment with music. They also have a well-designed program and high quality classes teachings in a variety of locations including Chicago, Downers Grove, Evanston, Lake Bluff, Lake forest, Wheaton, and Winnetka. More importnanty they offer an opportunity to learn and play orchestra or band instruments.
