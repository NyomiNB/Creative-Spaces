Wynwood Walls

Graffiti

Wynwood Walls is an organization located in Miami that has over 80,000 square feet of walls with graffiti art. It supports artists from many different countries. The street art brings life to the city more foot traffic, and income. It is a very picturesque location that also has a foundation for an artist program.
